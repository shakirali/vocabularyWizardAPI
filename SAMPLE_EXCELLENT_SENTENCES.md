## Common Quality Features

All excellent sentences demonstrate:

1. **Clear Contextual Clues**
   - Specific scenarios provide meaning hints
   - Cause-effect relationships evident
   - Descriptive language supports understanding

2. **Age-Appropriate Content**
   - Familiar situations (school, sports, home, nature)
   - Relatable characters and scenarios
   - Appropriate complexity for 10-11 year olds

3. **Natural Language**
   - Sentences sound natural when read aloud
   - Grammatically correct
   - British English spelling throughout

4. **Word-Specific Tailoring**
   - Each sentence crafted for its specific word
   - No generic templates
   - Demonstrates proper word usage

5. **Educational Value**
   - Students can infer meaning from context
   - Teaches vocabulary through natural usage
   - Prepares for actual reading comprehension

## Comparison: Generic vs Excellent

```

```
**Problem:** Same template for different words, no contextual clues, meaningless

