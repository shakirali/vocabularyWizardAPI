## üéØ QUICK QUALITY CHECKLIST

Use this checklist when reviewing or creating vocabulary entries:

### Meaning Quality
- [ ] Is it at least 30 characters long?
- [ ] Does it explain both definition AND usage context?
- [ ] Is it appropriate for the target age group?
- [ ] Does it avoid circular definitions?

### Synonyms
- [ ] Are both synonyms appropriate and accurate?
- [ ] Do they add information (not just repeat the meaning)?
- [ ] Are they at an appropriate difficulty level?
- [ ] Do they represent the word accurately?

### Antonyms
- [ ] Are both antonyms true opposites?
- [ ] Are they clear and unambiguous?
- [ ] Do they help clarify the word's meaning?
- [ ] Are they age-appropriate?

### Example Sentence
- [ ] Does it use the EXACT word form?
- [ ] Is it 30+ characters (substantial)?
- [ ] Does it start with a capital letter?
- [ ] Does it end with proper punctuation?
- [ ] Is it age-appropriate and relatable?
- [ ] Does it show the word in meaningful context?
- [ ] Could a student infer the meaning from context?
